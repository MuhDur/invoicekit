# Global E-Invoicing Regulatory Deadline & Format Map

*Compiled: May 2026. Verify against authoritative sources before product decisions — the regulatory landscape moves quarterly.*

This map covers every major jurisdiction with an active, phased, or announced e-invoicing mandate. Status, dates, formats, transmission channels, signing, archival, validation, CTC model, penalties, and authoritative source are recorded per country. Where dates have slipped or moved (France 2024 -> Sep 2026; Spain Royal Decree -> Jul 2027; Philippines -> Jan 2027; UAE -> Oct 2026 pilot), the *current* schedule is reflected.

## 2. Per-Country Detail

### EU bloc

#### EU ViDA (umbrella)
- **Status**: Adopted 11 Mar 2025; in force 14 Apr 2025. Member States may now introduce mandatory B2B e-invoicing without prior Commission derogation.
- **Key dates**: 1 Jul 2028 platform/SVR reforms; **1 Jul 2030 intra-EU B2B e-invoicing + Digital Reporting Requirements (DRR) mandatory**; 1 Jan 2035 domestic systems must align with EU model.
- **Format**: EN 16931-compliant (UBL 2.1 or UN/CEFACT CII). Future ViDA-aligned profile to follow EN 16931-1:2026 revision.
- **Channel**: Decentralised (4/5-corner) recommended; national portals tolerated until 2035 alignment.
- **Source**: https://taxation-customs.ec.europa.eu/taxation/vat/vat-digital-age-vida_en

#### Germany
- **Status**: Live (phased). Receive-only mandate active since 1 Jan 2025.
- **B2G**: Mandatory since 2020 (federal), per state for the rest.
- **B2B**: R mandatory 1 Jan 2025. I mandatory **1 Jan 2027 (turnover > €800k FY26)**, **1 Jan 2028 (All)**. EDI permitted during transition with bilateral agreement. *Confirmed schedule.*
- **B2C**: Not in scope.
- **Format**: XRechnung (CIUS UBL/CII), ZUGFeRD/Factur-X hybrid PDF+XML (CII), Peppol BIS — all EN 16931. From Jan 2027, EN 16931-conformant structured only.
- **Channel**: Decentralised, post-audit (no clearance). Email/portal/EDI/Peppol all acceptable.
- **Signing**: No signature required; integrity via business controls.
- **Archival**: 8 years (GoBD); original structured format.
- **Validation**: KoSIT XRechnung validator authoritative; Factur-X reference tools.
- **CTC**: None yet (post-audit). E-reporting to follow (ViDA-aligned, post-2028).
- **Penalties**: VAT deduction denial, general fiscal penalties.
- **Source**: https://ec.europa.eu/digital-building-blocks/sites/spaces/DIGITAL/pages/467108886/eInvoicing+in+Germany

#### France
- **Status**: Phased — confirmed schedule after the 2023 postponement.
- **B2G**: Mandatory via Chorus Pro since 2017 (Factur-X / UBL / CII).
- **B2B**: **1 Sep 2026** large + ETI must issue & receive; SME/TPE must receive. **1 Sep 2027** SME/TPE must issue. *Confirmed; PDP pilot started.*
- **B2C + cross-border**: E-reporting on the same timeline.
- **Format**: Factur-X (default), UBL 2.1, UN/CEFACT CII — all EN 16931.
- **Channel**: 4-corner via **PDP (Plateforme de Dématérialisation Partenaire)** accredited platforms, replacing the abandoned central PPF as the invoicing hub. PPF retained as registry/directory.
- **Signing**: Not required (PDP guarantees integrity).
- **Archival**: 10 years.
- **Validation**: PDP-internal; PPF reference annuaire/registry.
- **CTC**: Yes — clearance via PDP + e-reporting to DGFiP.
- **Penalties**: €15 per missing invoice (max €15k/yr) for invoicing breach; €250 per missing e-report (max €45k/yr).
- **Source**: https://ec.europa.eu/digital-building-blocks/sites/spaces/DIGITAL/pages/467108885/eInvoicing+in+France

#### Italy
- **Status**: Live for all (since 2019); fully matured.
- **B2G/B2B/B2C**: All in scope, including flat-rate < €25k since 2024.
- **Format**: **FatturaPA** (proprietary XML, EN 16931-aligned).
- **Channel**: **SDI (Sistema di Interscambio)** — mandatory clearance node operated by Agenzia delle Entrate.
- **Signing**: Qualified electronic signature (XAdES) required.
- **Archival**: 10 years; SDI conservazione sostitutiva.
- **Validation**: SDI = authoritative validator. Invoice has no legal value until SDI accepts.
- **CTC**: Yes — clearance.
- **Cross-border**: Since 1 Jul 2022, foreign-counterparty transactions reported to SDI (esterometro replacement).
- **Penalties**: 90–180% of VAT for non-issuance.
- **Source**: https://ec.europa.eu/digital-building-blocks/sites/spaces/DIGITAL/pages/467108890/eInvoicing+in+Italy

#### Spain
- **Status**: Two parallel tracks; both phased.
- **Track 1 — VeriFactu (RD 1007/2023)**: invoicing software requirements (immutability, chained hash "huella", QR). **Mandatory 1 Jan 2027** (CIT taxpayers) and **1 Jul 2027** (all others). Postponed from 2025/2026.
- **Track 2 — Crea y Crece (Law 18/2022)**: Structured B2B e-invoicing. Final regulation pending; expected dates **1 Jul 2027 (>€8m)** then **mid-2028 (SME)** counted 12/24 months from secondary regulation publication.
- **B2G**: FACe portal (FacturaE XML), since 2015. Mandatory above €5k.
- **Regional**: **TicketBAI** in Basque Country (Bizkaia/Gipuzkoa/Araba) fully live. **SII** for monthly VAT books since 2017 for large filers.
- **Format**: FacturaE 3.2.x (national); EN 16931 (UBL/CII) accepted under Crea y Crece.
- **Channel**: B2G via FACe/FACeB2B; B2B model is hybrid 4-corner with AEAT shadow copy.
- **Signing**: FacturaE XAdES e-signature mandatory.
- **Archival**: 4 years (commercial), 6 years (commercial code).
- **Penalties**: 1–2% of transaction; up to 75% for fraud; software breach up to €50k user / €150k vendor.
- **Source**: https://ec.europa.eu/digital-building-blocks/sites/spaces/DIGITAL/pages/467108901/eInvoicing+in+Spain

#### Poland
- **Status**: Phased; FA(3) schema replaces FA(2) at the cutover.
- **B2B**: **1 Feb 2026** taxpayers with FY25 turnover > **PLN 200m**; **1 Apr 2026** all other established taxpayers; **1 Jan 2027** micro-enterprises (where exempted earlier).
- **Receive**: All entrepreneurs must be able to receive KSeF invoices from 1 Feb 2026.
- **Format**: **FA(3) XML** (national schema).
- **Channel**: **KSeF (Krajowy System e-Faktur)** — government clearance portal. Invoice not legally issued until KSeF returns a KSeF ID.
- **Signing**: Authenticated session (e-signature, qualified seal, ePUAP, or token).
- **Archival**: 10 years in KSeF (system retains).
- **CTC**: Clearance.
- **Penalties**: Up to 100% of VAT on the invoice (sanctions phased in after initial leniency).
- **Source**: https://www.podatki.gov.pl/ksef/

#### Belgium
- **Status**: Live from 1 Jan 2026 (with three-month tolerance to 31 Mar 2026).
- **B2B**: Mandatory I+R for all Belgian VAT-liable enterprises (with limited exceptions — flat-rate farmers, exempt Art. 44, bankrupt).
- **B2G**: Already Peppol-mandatory (Mercurius platform).
- **Format**: **Peppol BIS Billing 3.0** (Belgium CIUS) — UBL 2.1 binding.
- **Channel**: **Peppol 4-corner exclusively** (no national clearance portal). Alternative formats allowed only with bilateral agreement, must still be EN 16931-compliant.
- **Signing**: Not required (Peppol AS4 transport provides integrity).
- **Archival**: 10 years.
- **CTC**: None initially; **e-reporting mandate targeted for 1 Jan 2028**.
- **Penalties**: Phased; €1,500 first / €3,000 second / €5,000 third per noncompliance after grace period.
- **Source**: https://finance.belgium.be/

#### Romania
- **Status**: Mature (live).
- **B2G/B2B/B2C**: B2B mandatory I via RO e-Factura since 1 Jul 2024 (resident + non-resident with VAT registration); B2C added 1 Jan 2025. Scope expanded 2026 to CNP-registered individuals.
- **Format**: **RO_CIUS** (UBL 2.1 — EN 16931 CIUS).
- **Channel**: **RO e-Factura** portal (ANAF clearance). 5 working days to submit.
- **Adjacent**: **RO e-Transport** (high-risk goods → all transports), **SAF-T D406** monthly.
- **Signing**: ANAF e-seal applied on acceptance.
- **Archival**: 5–10 years (corporate vs VAT).
- **Penalties**: 15% of invoice value for buyers accepting non-eFactura invoices; RON 5–10k per missed transmission.
- **Source**: https://www.anaf.ro/

#### Hungary
- **Status**: RTIR live since 2018. Future shift to full e-invoicing under consultation (closed 20 Jan 2026).
- **B2B/B2C/B2G**: RTIR via Online Számla — invoice data within 24h, real-time for B2B above thresholds.
- **2026 events**: Water-utility B2B e-invoicing mandatory 1 Jan; **receipt data via NAV from 1 Sep 2026**.
- **Format**: NAV XML schema (proprietary); planned shift to EN 16931/Peppol BIS in 2028+ pilot.
- **Channel**: NAV portal/API (post-issuance reporting, not clearance).
- **Signing**: Optional.
- **Archival**: 8 years.
- **CTC**: Real-time reporting (not clearance).
- **Penalties**: Up to HUF 500k per invoice not reported.
- **Source**: https://onlineszamla.nav.gov.hu/

#### Greece
- **Status**: Phased.
- **B2G**: Fully mandatory since 1 Sep 2025 (contracts > €2,500). Routed via myDATA → Peppol BIS 3.0 (Greece CIUS).
- **B2B**: **2 Mar 2026** businesses with FY23 gross > €1m (transition to 3 May); **1 Oct 2026** all others (transition to 31 Dec 2026). Domestic mandatory; intra-EU optional; non-EU mandatory.
- **Format**: myDATA XML / Peppol BIS 3.0 Greece CIUS.
- **Channel**: **myDATA (AADE)** central clearance; Peppol delivery for B2G; **EDIPs** (private providers) or free **timologio/myDATAapp**.
- **Signing**: AADE MARK + UID assigned at clearance.
- **Archival**: 5 years.
- **CTC**: Clearance.
- **Penalties**: 50% of VAT (min €250 / €500), doubled on repeat.
- **Source**: https://www.aade.gr/mydata

#### Portugal
- **Status**: Repeatedly postponed.
- **B2G**: Large since 2021; **SME/micro 1 Jan 2026**.
- **B2B/B2C**: PDF invoices accepted (without QES) through 2026 if issued by AT-certified software with ATCUD + QR. **QES mandatory 1 Jan 2027**.
- **SAF-T**: Billing monthly (live). **Accounting SAF-T-PT** first submission 2028 (FY27).
- **Format**: CIUS-PT (UBL 2.1) for structured B2B; SAF-T-PT XML for periodic.
- **Channel**: Portal das Finanças / AT-certified software issuing ATCUD.
- **Signing**: QES for PDF from 2027 (or structured CIUS-PT / approved EDI).
- **Archival**: 10 years.
- **CTC**: Periodic (post-audit) via SAF-T Billing.
- **Penalties**: €200–€10k.
- **Source**: https://info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt/

#### Netherlands
- **Status**: B2G Peppol strongly recommended; no domestic B2B mandate planned before EU DRR 2030.
- **Format**: NLCIUS (Peppol BIS 3.0 CIUS), SI-UBL, UBL-OHNL.
- **Channel**: Digipoort (B2G), Peppol.
- **Signing**: None required.
- **Archival**: 7 years.
- **Source**: https://www.peppolautoriteit.nl/

#### Denmark

- **Format**: **NemHandel BIS 4** = adapted Peppol BIS 4 (replacing OIOUBL 2.1 / OIOUBL 3.0).
- **Channel**: NemHandel / Peppol.
- **Archival**: 5 years.
- **Source**: https://erhvervsstyrelsen.dk/

## 6. Format zoo: how many formats does a "global" product need?

If you treat every distinct **on-wire syntax** that a tax authority *mandates* as a distinct target, a global product needs to handle the following count:

**EN 16931-aligned (the convergent set — ~70% of mandates):**
- UBL 2.1 (Peppol BIS 3.0; PINT AE / A-NZ / SG / JP) — 1 syntactic family, ~12 CIUS variants.
- UBL 2.1 + Peppol BIS 4.0 — emerging.
- UN/CEFACT CII (XRechnung CII binding, ZUGFeRD / Factur-X) — 1 syntactic family.

**Within EN 16931 you must handle ~20 CIUS profiles** (XRechnung, ZUGFeRD, Factur-X, Peppol BIS 3.0 + the country CIUSs for BE/IT-FatturaPA-aligned/NL/DK/HR/PT/SE/FI/NO/IS/LU/PL future/RO/SI/SK/UAE/AU-NZ/SG/JP). Most of these differ only in mandatory business rules (`BR-DE-*`, `BR-NL-*`, `BR-CO-*`...) and code lists — same backbone, different validation surface.

**Non-EN 16931 mandated formats (the irreducible specials):**
1. FatturaPA (Italy)
2. KSeF FA(3) (Poland)
3. FacturaE 3.2.x (Spain B2G — eclipsed by Crea y Crece UBL but still required for FACe)
4. CFDI 4.0 + complementos (Mexico) — *plus* 30+ industry-specific complementos
5. NF-e (Brazil)
6. NFC-e (Brazil)
7. NFS-e padronizada (Brazil services)
8. NFCom (Brazil telecoms)
9. CT-e + CT-e OS (Brazil transport)
10. MDF-e, BP-e, NF3e (Brazil — niche)
11. DTE (Chile)
12. e-CF (Dominican Republic)
13. UBL-TR / e-Fatura (Turkey)
14. UBL-TR / e-Arşiv (Turkey)
15. SUNAT UBL (Peru — Peppol-influenced but not strict)
16. DIAN UBL (Colombia — Peppol-influenced but not strict)
17. SRI XML (Ecuador)
18. Hacienda XML (Costa Rica)
19. ARCA XML (Argentina)
20. ETA JSON/XML (Egypt)
21. JoFotara JSON (Jordan)
22. ZATCA UBL CIUS (Saudi — actually EN 16931-aligned, sometimes counted as convergent)
23. MyInvois UBL/JSON (Malaysia — UBL but custom 55-field profile)
24. EIS JSON (Philippines)
25. e-Faktur XML (Indonesia)
26. NTS XML (South Korea)
27. JP PINT (Japan — actually EN 16931-aligned via PINT)
28. e-Fapiao XML (China)
29. KRA TIMS XML (Kenya)
30. FIRS UBL (Nigeria — EN 16931-aligned via Peppol)
31. ISDOC (Czechia — fading)
32. INV-01 JSON (India)
33. GST QR (India B2C QR variant)
34. myDATA XML (Greece — aligned but proprietary)

**Convergence trend**: In 2024–2026, every *new* mandate (BE, HR, SK, SI, NO, NZ, AU update, SG, JP, UAE, ZATCA, FIRS) chose **UBL 2.1 + EN 16931 + Peppol** as its backbone. The non-EN16931 specials (Italy, Poland, Spain B2G, all of LatAm except Colombia/Peru/Nigeria-style UBL, all of Asia except SG/JP, China) are legacy — and ViDA 2030 + EN 16931-1:2026 will force EU-side migration into a single semantic model. Italy is publicly investigating ViDA-alignment for FatturaPA; LatAm is unlikely to converge before 2035.

**Bottom line for a global product**: support **EN 16931 (UBL + CII) + ~5 LatAm proprietary (CFDI, NF-e family, DTE, e-CF, ARCA) + China e-Fapiao + a small set of MENA/APAC JSON/XML profiles** ≈ ~15 *primary* engines, each with ~2-10 variants. Don't be fooled by the long list — under ViDA the convergent set keeps growing.
